LowLatencyToolkit

A modular PowerShell automation toolkit that applies BIOS/OS performance profiles to Windows gaming systems. Five profiles from basic service cleanup to full Fortnite optimization, with idempotent execution, GPU auto-detection (Nvidia/AMD), full rollback support via restore points and snapshots, and structured logging for every change. Built to reduce input latency and maximize frame rates without breaking your system.

CardSlinger

A Discord bot that manages and distributes cards (VCCs, gift cards, promo codes, license keys) to teams through slash commands. One card at a time per user with built-in safeguards. Used cards are never recycled, "not used" requires confirmation, error cards auto-replace. Includes CSV bulk loading, admin controls, low stock alerts, activity logging, and multi-server support. Single-file architecture with Docker deployment.
